Me, Myself & I

Student & Up-And-Coming Programmer

Hello! I'm Jacob, a (relatively new) programmer who is currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the University of Maryland. Ever since early childhood, I've yearned to become a professional programmer due to my love of video games. The process of developers bringing their creativity to life absolutely fascinated me in a way like no other, and I knew it was what I wanted to do.

Though I did not start coding seriously until last year, my passion for programming has grown larger in that time. Java is currently my strongest language, but I have continued to study others such as C# and HTML to expand my knowledge and versatiity, and am willing to tackle whatever challenges come my way.

I am still growing as a programmer, and hope to continue that growth by applying my passion and drive to any company that's willing to employ me. A hard-worker, team player, and self-starter, I hope to be given the chance to learn from my peers and prove myself as a professional programmer, one line of code at a time.
